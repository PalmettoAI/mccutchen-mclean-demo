### 1. "Your intake process is losing you cases before you even hear about them"
When someone faces a legal issue, they search online at 10pm. If McCutchen McLean's site doesn't have a form that sends an instant confirmation, that prospect moves to the next firm. An automated intake system captures them at their most motivated moment.

### 2. "30 years of trust shouldn't be buried in 2005 web design"
A firm established in 1995 has earned its reputation. The demo gives it a presence that matches. Ask: "When someone in Lexington Googles 'real estate attorney Lexington SC,' do you show up — and does the site they land on close them?"

### 3. "Automated follow-up means no lead goes cold"
After someone submits a consultation request, they typically call 2–3 other firms. The demo triggers an immediate confirmation text and a follow-up reminder — so McCutchen McLean is the first voice they hear back from.

### 4. Estate planning and real estate are evergreen, high-value practice areas
Lexington is a growing market. Real estate closings, wills, trusts, business formation — all steady-demand services. A site that captures those searches is a reliable pipeline.
